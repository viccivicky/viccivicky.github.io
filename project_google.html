Context

Autonomous Units are independent divisions within Google who have the freedom to run like a startup. I was working on the social gaming segment of a start-up called Slide which was founded by one of the co-founders of PayPal - Max Levchin. When Google made the accqsition the goal was to help them shape the social part of their business.

Product scope

The most pular product was a pet caring game called SuperPoke Pets. Our team was responsible for the spin off version called SuperPoke Pets - Ranch. This was a brand new game where players are able to build a farm and raise animal characters from SuperPoke Pets. I was responsible for creating a of the characters, game sets and graphical interfaces.
